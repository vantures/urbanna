Rivah Race

Navigate the river, collect oysters, dodge danger!

Throttle up and dash down Virginia's Rappahannock River, starting in the shadow of historic Fredericksburg, sweeping past Tappahannock's old wharves, skimming by the charming port of Urbanna, and finally roaring into the open waters where the Rivah meets the Chesapeake Bay. Grab shimmering magic oysters, weave through river traffic, and see how far you can ride the current!

How to Play

  • Move left/right with the arrow keys or by dragging on touch.
  • Collect oysters for 100 bonus points & temporary invincibility.
  • Avoid logs, buoys, kayakers, geese, jet-skis, and osprey nests.
  • The river speeds up the longer you survive. How far can you go?
